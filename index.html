BREAKING: Shooting / Security Incident Reported at White House Correspondents’ Dinner

Breaking News Desk • Washington, D.C. • Developing

Multiple major outlets are reporting that the White House Correspondents’ Dinner at the Washington Hilton was disrupted by a shooting or security incident Saturday night. Early reports say President Donald Trump, the First Lady, Vice President JD Vance, and senior officials were evacuated or secured by law enforcement.

Reuters and AP report that a suspect was taken into custody and that federal law enforcement responded quickly. Early reporting says one officer or agent may have been struck but was protected by body armor. Details on the suspect, motive, exact timeline, and injuries are still developing.

Editorial standard: This is a developing story. BlackSheep Patriots News is treating all early claims cautiously until confirmed by official statements from the Secret Service, FBI, White House, or D.C. law enforcement.

Sources: Reuters • Associated Press • Fox News live updates
