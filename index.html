Atlantic City Watch: Atlantic Avenue paving enters final phase

Local Brief • Source: Press of Atlantic City • April 2026

Atlantic City infrastructure remains a major local story. Press of Atlantic City reported that contractors started the final phase of a $24.8 million Atlantic Avenue repaving project, according to city officials. Roadwork like this affects drivers, businesses, residents, tourists, emergency access, and the image of the city.
